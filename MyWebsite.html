Iron Maiden

Over 40 years of god-tier metal.

Iron Maiden, founded in 1975, were pioneers of heavy metal in the genre's infancy. The unique sound they possessed and their intensity has led them to become one of the most successful heavy metal bands of all time. Despite a lack of radio or television exposure throughout their career, the band has achieved a massive following, and has sold over 90 million records worldwide to date. They're doing something right.
